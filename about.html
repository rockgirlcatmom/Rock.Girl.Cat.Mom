Collected with intention

Hi, I’m Rebecca — a lifelong collector, cat mom, creative spirit, and lover of quiet beauty.

I recently moved to St. Catharines and fell instantly in love with the Niagara region — the water, the stone, the history, the energy. Exploring beaches, trails, and forgotten places has become part of my everyday inspiration.

My world is shaped by small details: a perfectly worn stone, a soft color palette, natural textures, and moments that feel grounding. I believe beauty doesn’t need to be loud — it just needs to be intentional.

When I’m not collecting or creating, I’m usually surrounded by my five cats — Olive, Puzzle, Finn, Nala, and Smokey — who constantly remind me to slow down and notice the little things. I also carry the memory of Caesar with me always.

This space is a reflection of what I love: curated finds, quiet luxury, soft color, and a life built gently and with care.

— Rebecca
