About Me

I’m Rebecca — a rock-loving, cat-obsessed creative who just moved to St. Catharines and instantly fell in love with the Niagara region. I collect stones, fossils, and tiny pieces of Earth that feel meaningful, calming, and beautiful. Everything here is chosen slowly, intentionally, and with heart.
