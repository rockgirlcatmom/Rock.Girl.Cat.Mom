About Me

I collect what speaks to me. Not just stones — but moments, textures, light, stories, and pieces of nature that feel meaningful.

Rock Girl Cat Mom is a space for things collected with intention.

From shoreline stones and quiet views, to creative projects, memories, and moments that make me pause — everything here is chosen because it carries feeling.

I believe nature holds stories if you slow down enough to notice them. Some become art. Some become treasures. Some simply become part of the journey.

This space is where I share those finds — thoughtfully, honestly, and with intention.

Welcome to my collection.
